Notes

  • The Core Seasonal Monsters were the first monsters whose elements were introduced after the monsters themselves. Before June 25th, 2016, they used to have no element at all, though they were shown only in news promotions and were present in My Singing Monsters Official Guide. In update 3.0.4, their elements appeared in-game.
  • Kayna is the only monster that has different breeding behavior depending on the MSM game that it is in. This makes it, in a way, the only monster with two possible Breeding Classes.
  • Dipsters used to have no elements, but have been given one since June 2017. Like Seasonals, their Element didn’t appear in-game until update 3.0.4.
  • Some magical elements are extremely similar to symbols that already existed within the game, such as the Psychic and Bone element, which are "present" in the Wubbox Stockpile.
